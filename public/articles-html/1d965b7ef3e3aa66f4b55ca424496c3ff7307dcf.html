The Yankees squeeze past the Red Sox while the Mets strike out: The nation's favorite baseball teams revealed

  • A new Facebook graph reveals the favorite baseball teams in the country by sorting through people's likes and breaking down winners by county
  • The New York Yankees and Boston Red Sox, who come in first and second respectively in terms of the number of fans
  • The New York Mets and Oakland Athletics did not win one single county 

By Chris Spargo For Dailymail.com

Published: 13:01 EST, 4 April 2015 | Updated: 05:26 EST, 5 April 2015

Baseball has long been considered America's favorite pastime, so it should come as no surprise that most people feel very strongly when it comes to their favorite professional team. 

And there are no two baseball teams that are greater, or more storied, than eternal rivals the New York Yankees and Boston Red Sox, who come in first and second respectively in terms of the number of people who claim them as their favorite.

Also making a very impressive showing are the Texas Rangers and Atlanta Braves, who have fans across a wide area in the South.

Then there are a few teams who don't get as much love - like the New York Mets.

The Mets are not the favorite team in one county in the country, with fans in their area seeming to all go for the Yankees.
